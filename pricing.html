COMMON QUESTIONS
What counts as a "clip"?
Each individual clip cut from your video counts as one clip. If you request 3 clips from a YouTube video, that uses 3 of your daily allowance.
When does my daily limit reset?
Free plan limits reset at midnight UTC every day. Pro, Creator, and Business plans have no daily limits.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your Stripe billing portal and your plan reverts to Free at the end of the billing period. No questions asked.
What is the yearly plan?
Yearly billing gives you 2 months free compared to paying monthly. You're billed once per year for the full amount.
What is the API key used for?
Business subscribers get a unique API key for direct integration with your own tools and workflows. Full API docs coming soon.
Are my clips stored permanently?
Clips are stored temporarily on the server and cleared periodically. Download your clips right after generating them.
