Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

ClipCut collects minimal data necessary to operate the Service:

  • Session data: An anonymous session ID stored in a cookie to track your daily clip usage
  • Email address: Only collected when you subscribe to a paid plan via our payment processor
  • Payment data: Handled entirely by Paddle — we never see or store your card details
  • Usage data: Number of clips generated per day (reset daily)

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To enforce plan limits and track daily clip usage
  • To manage your subscription status
  • To provide customer support

3. Data Storage

Session and usage data is stored in a secure database on our servers. Video files are stored temporarily during processing and deleted automatically. We do not retain your video content.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Paddle: Our payment processor. Paddle's privacy policy applies to all payment transactions.
  • YouTube / yt-dlp: Used to fetch publicly available video content per YouTube's Terms of Service.

5. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to identify your browser session. No tracking or advertising cookies are used.

6. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us. For paid subscribers, cancelling your plan removes your email from active records.

7. Data Retention

Anonymous session data is retained for 90 days. Email and subscription data is retained while your account is active and for 30 days after cancellation.

8. Children's Privacy

ClipCut is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Contact

Privacy questions: support@clipcut.app
