Refund Policy

Last updated: April 28, 2026

We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on all paid plans. If you're not satisfied, contact us within 7 days of your first payment for a full refund.

Monthly Plans

If you subscribed to a monthly plan and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 7 days of your initial payment. Refunds are not available for subsequent monthly renewals — please cancel before your renewal date to avoid being charged.

Annual Plans

If you subscribed to an annual plan and are not satisfied, you may request a full refund within 7 days of payment. After 7 days, annual plans are non-refundable but you will retain access until the end of the period.

How to Request a Refund

Email us at support@clipcut.app with:

  • Your email address used to subscribe
  • The reason for your refund request

We process refunds within 3–5 business days. The refund will appear on your original payment method.

Cancellations

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation stops future charges. Your plan remains active until the end of the current billing period. Cancellation does not automatically trigger a refund.

Exceptions

Refunds may be declined if there is evidence of abuse of the Service or if this is not your first refund request.

Contact

Questions about refunds: support@clipcut.app
